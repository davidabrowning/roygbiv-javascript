How to play

Sort your 10-card hand in ascending order.

  1. Choose a player 1 (top row) and player 2 (bottom row).
  2. Each player swaps the positions of two cards within their hand.
  3. Take turns drawing a card from either the draw or discard pile and replacing a card in your hand with it.
  4. When all of your cards are in ascending order you win!
